About Us

OUR MISSION

Our mission is to empower youth to lead real change in their communities by providing free tutoring, affordable care services, and hands-on outreach programs. We believe that every young person has the power to make a difference when given the chance to lead, serve, and build. Through our work, we aim to support families, uplift neighbors, and create lasting impact across Northern Virginia.

HOW WE STARTED

NOVAStrong was started in 2024 by a group of high school students in Northern Virginia who wanted to give back to their community. What began as a small effort to provide free tutoring and support has grown into a youth-led movement focused on service, leadership, and impact. Today, our programs continue to empower young people to take action and lead projects that uplift families, seniors, and neighborhoods across the region.
