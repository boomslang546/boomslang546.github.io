Awards & Grants

Recognizing partners that help power our impact.

Pollination Project Grant

This grant supports youth-led initiatives that create practical change in local communities. Funding from the Pollination Project helps NOVAStrong accelerate early-stage ideas into real, on-the-ground impact across Northern Virginia.

Home Depot Foundation Grant

Support from the Home Depot Foundation fuels hands-on service projects—improving spaces, building resources, and supplying materials for neighborhood-focused efforts that strengthen families and seniors in NOVA.
